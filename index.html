How this course works
I

Foundations First

We start from the ground up — web fundamentals, frontend, backend, databases, APIs, version control, and how the internet actually works. No shortcuts, no assumed knowledge.

II

Pick Your Problem

You identify a real-world problem you care about. Then you design the solution. Tech stack is your call — React, Vue, Node, Python, or whatever fits the problem best.

III

Build & Deploy

You ship your project live to the internet. Real domain, real users, real feedback. This becomes the centerpiece of your portfolio.

IV

Map Your Future

You create your own personalized learning roadmap — what to learn next, what to specialize in, and how to grow as an engineer from here.
